Refund Policy

Please review the refund conditions before making payment.

  • Tickets once booked are generally non-refundable.
  • Refund may be considered only if event is cancelled by organizer.
  • No refund for no-show, late arrival, or duplicate bookings.
  • In approved refund cases, amount is processed to original payment method.
  • Processing timelines may vary based on payment provider policies.
